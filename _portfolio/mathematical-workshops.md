## Overview

The Mathematical Workshops are a series of articles where I explore mathematical problems through detailed, step-by-step solutions.

The goal is to build a public trace of my mathematical thinking, improve my exposition skills, and develop a strong portfolio for future research opportunities.

## Purpose

This project is part of a broader effort to become a stronger independent mathematical thinker. Each workshop is designed to be readable, rigorous, and useful for someone trying to understand not only the solution, but also the strategy behind it.

## Topics

The series includes problems from areas such as:

- Algebra
- Combinatorics
- Number theory
- Probability
- Inequalities
- Mathematical games
- Generating functions
